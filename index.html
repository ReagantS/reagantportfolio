About Me

I am a student at the University of Pelita Harapan Medan, currently enrolled as part of the Class of 2023 pursuing a degree in Information Systems. As a passionate learner and team player, I chose to step into the role of a mentor during the arrival of the Class of 2024. This role allows me to introduce them to the variety of mentoring programs and opportunities available.

My mission is to support new students as they adjust to university life by offering guidance and sharing knowledge to ensure their success. Through my mentoring journey, I aim to build a community of collaborative, inspired, and motivated learners.
